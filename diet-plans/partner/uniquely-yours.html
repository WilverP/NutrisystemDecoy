Here’s what’s included in your 2-week Partner Plan

The Partner Plan includes two weeks of Nutrisystem® meals and snacks for two people.

Here’s a breakdown of your first shipment:

  • Week 1: Reboot Your Bodies

You and your weight loss partner will kickstart your weight loss with 7 full days of Nutrisystem meals and protein shakes, designed to activate your body’s fat-burning mode.

  • Week 2+: Steady Weight Loss

Starting with week two, you will each enjoy your choice of either top-rated frozen and ready-to-go Nutrisystem® meals and snacks (or both!) and a few of your own fresh grocery foods each day for the best nutritional balance.

You’ll both eat six times a day. Most days, women will enjoy four Nutrisystem meals a day: breakfast, lunch, dinner and a snack, plus two fresh grocery snacks. Since men require more calories, most days guys will enjoy five Nutrisystem meals a day: breakfast, lunch, dinner and two snacks, plus one fresh grocery snack.

For those on the 5-day plan, you’ll also add Flex meals to your meal plan throughout the week. Cook a meal together using our easy recipes—even relax with a glass of wine. You and your partner will each choose when to enjoy two Flex breakfasts, lunches, dinners and snacks each week.

  • PLUS!

You’ll both get unlimited support from our weight loss coaches by phone or online, any day of the week, and access to our FREE NuMi® app for additional motivation and support tools, weight loss challenges and amazing prizes!

Which Partner Plan is best for me?

When ordering your Partner Plan, you can choose our Uniquely Yours MAX+ or Uniquely Yours configuration. While both options are designed to deliver lasting weight loss for two people, menu varieties vary based on your selection. Here’s a simple breakdown of the differences between our Partner Plan configurations:

Partner Plan: Uniquely Yours MAX+ (160+ menu choices)
Featuring our biggest and best menu variety:

  • Grab-and-go meals and snacks — Pantry-ready Nutrisystem food, perfect for eating on-the-go.
  • Top-rated frozen meals and snacks — Our most popular heat-and-eat meals and even ice cream!
  • New big and filling premium meals — Hearty dinners with up to 30g protein to keep you feeling full and satisfied while you lose weight.

Why is this plan limited to two people in the same household?

The Partner Plan is designed for two people living in the same home. You will receive one shipment with two weeks of meals and snacks for two people in one package. Since the shipment can’t be divided and sent to two different addresses, it really works best for two people living together!

That means your husband or wife, partner, roommate, child over 18 years old, or any other family members living with you can become your partner in weight loss—and you’ll both get to experience the benefits of losing weight together!

What if we have a special diet? (Diabetes, vegetarian, etc.)

Nutrisystem works for a variety of dietary needs.

Following a low-sodium diet? All of our plans meet, and are often below, the USDA's recommended daily intake of 2,300mg sodium or less for the general population. For those who need to limit sodium to 1,500mg per day, our weight loss coaches can help customize your menu to meet those needs.

Special dietary needs or food allergies? You can find a nutritional breakdown and ingredient lists for all of our menu items online. We recommend choosing your own menu, so that you can use the menu filters to exclude any ingredients that you need to avoid. Filters can be used to view diabetes-friendly foods, vegetarian foods, or to exclude foods that contain milk, wheat, nuts and more. If you and your weight loss partner are both vegetarian, we recommend our 4-week Vegetarian Plan instead.

Need help navigating the menu? Our nutrition and dietary services team can help select foods that work for you and your weight loss partner’s needs.

What makes the Nutrisystem foods healthier?

On Nutrisystem, you can lose weight eating the foods you love, made healthier. So what does that mean?

The Nutrisystem program meets, or in many cases exceeds, most of the recommendations set forth by the government in the 2015-2020 Dietary Guidelines for Americans.

Our plans are designed to include:

  • The right mix of nutrients to fuel your body
  • Plenty of healthy, lean protein
  • High fiber to help keep you feeling fuller, longer
  • Low-glycemic carbs to stabilize blood sugar
  • No artificial sweeteners or flavors

How is our order delivered? When will it arrive?

We’re taking steps to ensure that minimal contact is required for our shipments. Your order will be delivered safely to your doorstep. You can expect delivery in 1 to 10 business days, with a majority of shipments arriving within 7 business days.

As always, you’ll receive FREE home delivery in the Continental U.S. only, on every plan shipment.

We’ll email you a tracking number when each order ships, so you can stay informed on the delivery date.

Any orders that contain frozen foods will be sent in two separate shipments: frozen foods and ready-to-go foods. Your frozen foods will be packed in a specially designed cooler with dry ice to keep everything frozen for approximately six hours upon delivery.
